Schistosomiasis has been my longest public health research endeavor since the 1980s, starting in Brazil, and China in the 1990s, at the time when disease prevalence or disease burdern were world's highest in both countries. In approximately one decade Brazil and China have emmerged from highest prevalence and disease burden to a status of experts informing and backing up WHO install global support and training on disease control, surveilance, and eradication. My research in schistosomiasis through the years are glimpses of major challenges and success case study from Brazil, China and the Philippines
